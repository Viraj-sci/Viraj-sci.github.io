Biotechnology Research

Explore my research projects and interests in the field of biotechnology.

Research Projects

Genetic Modification of Crop Plants

This project focuses on using CRISPR technology to enhance the resistance of staple crops to pests and diseases, thereby improving yield and sustainability in agriculture.

Development of Biodegradable Plastics

Investigating the production of biodegradable plastics from renewable resources, this research aims to reduce plastic pollution and promote sustainable materials.

Microbial Fuel Cells for Energy Production

This study explores the use of microbial fuel cells to generate electricity from organic waste, assessing their efficiency and potential for renewable energy applications.

Biopharmaceutical Production

Researching the use of recombinant DNA technology for the production of biopharmaceuticals, including monoclonal antibodies and vaccines, to improve therapeutic options.

CRISPR-Cas9 Gene Editing in Human Cells

This project aims to explore the application of CRISPR-Cas9 technology for correcting genetic mutations associated with inherited diseases.

Microbial Bioremediation of Heavy Metals

Investigating the potential of specific microbial strains to detoxify and remove heavy metals from contaminated soil and water sources.

Plant Tissue Culture Techniques

Exploring the methods of plant tissue culture to propagate rare and endangered plant species, contributing to biodiversity conservation.

Development of Novel Vaccines

This research focuses on the design and testing of new vaccines for infectious diseases, utilizing advanced delivery systems and adjuvants.
