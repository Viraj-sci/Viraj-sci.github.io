Explore my research projects and interests in the field of environmental biology.

Research Projects

Impact of Urbanization on Local Biodiversity

This project investigates how urban development affects local wildlife populations and plant diversity in city environments. We collected data on species richness and abundance in green spaces and compared them to less developed areas.

Effects of Pollution on Aquatic Ecosystems

This study examines the impact of industrial runoff on freshwater ecosystems. Water samples were analyzed for pollutants, and the health of aquatic organisms was assessed to determine the extent of ecological damage.

Sustainable Practices in Agriculture

Focusing on sustainable agriculture techniques, this project explored methods such as crop rotation, organic farming, and integrated pest management to enhance soil health and reduce environmental impact.

Climate Change Effects on Marine Life

This research analyzes how rising sea temperatures and ocean acidification affect marine biodiversity. The project includes field studies and laboratory experiments to assess the resilience of various species to changing conditions.
